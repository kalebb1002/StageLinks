About StageLinks


What We Do

Stagelinks is an open source passion project aimed at fostering connections within the community theater ecosystem.

About the Author

Stagelinks is developed and maintained by Kaleb Bauerlein, a software developer and theater enthusiast. With a background in both technology and the arts, Kaleb created Stagelinks to bridge the gap between these two worlds and provide a platform to facilitate connections between actors and theater companies.
